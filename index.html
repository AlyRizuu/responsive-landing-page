Frequently Asked Questions

What type of producer can join the Marketplace?

Independent producers, Non-Guild (Industry Professionals), Non-Guild (Financier/Investor), Member of ASC, Member of PGA, Member of DGA, Member of SAG/AFTRA, and Producers Union (New Association) are all eligible to join Wo Marketplace.

Will I have to apply to get access?

Yes! You have to apply to join our Marketplace. Your application will be reviewed and if approved based on our producer vetting standards, then you will be given full access.

Do I have to buy a material before joining the Marketplace?

No, you do not have to buy a material to join the marketplace. You can buy new material after joining the Marketplace.

What kind of scripts can I get in the Marketplace?

You will find scripts in the following: Genre: action, adventure, animated, comedy, drama, fantasy, foreign, historical, horror, science, thriller, and western. Ratings: G, PG, PG-13, and R . Timeline: past, present, and future.

Will I have to apply to get access?

You can choose the 6 months or 12 months option term, and after option expiration, extensions are allowed for 6 months (one time only).
